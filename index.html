Collapsible.css

Usage

<div data-collapsible>

    <input id="example2" type="checkbox" checked>
    <label for="example2" onclick>Show</label>

    <div class="collapsible-content">
        <strong>I'm always collapsible</strong>
        <p>

        </p>
    </div>

</div>
